We Are Proud Students & Alumni of Brandeis University

The Brandeis Students and Alumni Association is a vibrant community that offers lifelong opportunities for students and alumni to stay connected to Brandeis and each other.

Graduates of Brandeis International Business School are some of the most diverse and dynamic members of Brandeis University’s 60,000-person alumni network. As a graduate, you have access to a vibrant alumni group of innovators, mentors and global business leaders. We invite you to leverage your lifelong alumni benefits and reconnect with the International Business School today.
